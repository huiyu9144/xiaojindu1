🔒 Progress - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Progress ("this app") takes user privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how the app handles user data.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal user information, including but not limited to name, email address, phone number, location, etc.

Data Storage

📱 Local Storage
All app data (including project names, progress percentages, etc.) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.

🔒 Data Security
Your data stays entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any cloud server. Only you can access and manage this data.

Data Usage

This app uses user data solely to provide progress management functionality, including displaying project lists, saving progress information, etc.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising services, or data tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual projects within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all data

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Users will be notified within the app when updated.
